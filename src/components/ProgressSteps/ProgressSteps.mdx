## Examples

### Sizes

Use `size` to control the scale of step indicators.

<CollapsibleCanvas of={ProgressStepsStories.Sizes} />
<div className="canvas-controls-only">
<Controls of={ProgressStepsStories.Sizes} exclude={["total", "variant"]} />
</div>

### Vertical

Use `orientation="vertical"` to display steps in a vertical layout, ideal for sidebar navigation or multi-step forms. The example also shows the dashed connector style.

<CollapsibleCanvas of={ProgressStepsStories.Vertical} />
<CollapsibleSource of={ProgressStepsStories.Vertical} />

### With Icons

Use `icon` prop on `ProgressSteps.Step` to display custom icons instead of step numbers.

<CollapsibleCanvas of={ProgressStepsStories.WithIcons} />
<CollapsibleSource of={ProgressStepsStories.WithIcons} />

### With Navigation

Example of integrating with navigation controls.

<CollapsibleCanvas of={ProgressStepsStories.WithNavigation} />
<CollapsibleSource of={ProgressStepsStories.WithNavigation} />

### Minimal

Use `variant="minimal"` for a compact horizontal bar indicator. Requires `total` prop.

<CollapsibleCanvas of={ProgressStepsStories.Minimal} />
<div className="canvas-controls-only">
<Controls of={ProgressStepsStories.Minimal} include={["current", "color"]} />
</div>
<CollapsibleSource of={ProgressStepsStories.Minimal} />

### Icons Only

Omit `Title` and `Description` for a minimal indicator when step details are shown elsewhere.

<CollapsibleCanvas of={ProgressStepsStories.IconsOnly} />
<CollapsibleSource of={ProgressStepsStories.IconsOnly} />

### Colors

Use `color` to change the color scheme of completed and active steps.

<CollapsibleCanvas of={ProgressStepsStories.SuccessColor} />
